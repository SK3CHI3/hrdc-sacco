# 🎭 Kopa SACCO: Feature Walkthrough Guide

This guide provides a step-by-step narrative for a functional walkthrough of the platform. Focus on the **flow**—showing how one action triggers the next seamlessly.

---

## 🏁 Phase 1: Frictionless Access
**Goal**: Show how easy it is for any stakeholder to jump into the system.

1.  **The Login Experience**: 
    - *Action*: Go to the Login page.
    - *Narrative*: "We've built specialized portals for every role—Admins, Members, and Credit Officers. For this demo, we're using our dev-shortcuts to switch between these 'hats' instantly."
    - *Show*: The **Auto-fill** buttons for different roles.

---

## 📊 Phase 2: The Command Center (Member Dashboard)
**Goal**: Show transparency and "Spending Power."

1.  **Dashboard Stats**:
    - *Action*: Log in as an **Active Member**.
    - *Narrative*: "A member's first view is their financial health. Unlike traditional systems, we show the **Available Balance** vs. **Committed Balance**. If you've guaranteed a friend's loan, that money is 'locked' and tracked in real-time."
    - *Show*: The balance cards and the **"Can Borrow"** loan limit.

---

## 💸 Phase 3: The "Magic" Loan Workflow
**Goal**: Show the transition from manual hassle to digital speed.

1.  **Applying for a Loan**:
    - *Action*: Navigate to **Loans** → **Apply**.
    - *Narrative*: "Let's apply for KES 500,000. The system instantly calculates interest and monthly repayments. No wait times, no opaque math."
2.  **The Guarantor Search**:
    - *Action*: On the Loan Details page, click **Add Guarantor**.
    - *Narrative*: "This is the game-changer. I don't need a physical form. I search for a member by name, and send a digital request. This is **Social Collateral** in action."
3.  **Role Switch & Acceptance**:
    - *Action*: Log out, log in as **Committee Member** (or another member), and go to the **Guarantor Requests** page.
    - *Narrative*: "As a guarantor, I see exactly what I'm backing. I click **Accept**, and watch what happens next..."
4.  **Auto-Submission**:
    - *Action*: Switch back to the Borrower's loan page.
    - *Narrative*: "The moment the guarantor clicked 'Accept', the loan **automatically** moved to `SUBMITTED`. The system is now notifying the Credit Officer. The friction is gone."

---

## 🤝 Phase 4: Community & Growth
**Goal**: Show how the platform grows itself.

1.  **Referrals & Loyalty**:
    - *Action*: Navigate to the **Referrals** page.
    - *Narrative*: "Growth is built into the code. Members share their unique codes to earn loyalty points. This turns every user into a growth agent for the SACCO."
    - *Show*: The referral code and the points history.

---

## 🛡️ Phase 5: The Admin Control Tower
**Goal**: Show oversight and security.

1.  **Member Management**:
    - *Action*: Log in as **Sacco Admin** and go to **Member Management**.
    - *Narrative*: "Admins have a birds-eye view of every application. When I approve a member here, the system automatically triggers their welcome bonus and updates the entire referral chain."
    - *Show*: The list of pending members and the **Approve** button.

---

## 🎯 Wrap Up
"What you've seen is a complete financial ecosystem where trust is digitized, growth is automated, and every transaction is transparent. This is the future of community banking." 🚀
