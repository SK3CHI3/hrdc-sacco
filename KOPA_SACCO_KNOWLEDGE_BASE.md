# Kopa SACCO Support Agent Knowledge Base

## About Kopa SACCO

Kopa SACCO is a modern Savings and Credit Cooperative Society that provides digital financial services to members. Our platform enables members to save, apply for loans, and access financial services conveniently through our web-based portal.

## 1. MEMBERSHIP & JOINING KOPA SACCO

### How to Join Kopa SACCO

**Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be 18 years or older
- Valid Kenyan National ID
- Valid phone number and email address
- Passport-sized photo

**Registration Process:**
1. Visit our website and click "Register"
2. Create an account with your email and password
3. Complete the onboarding form with:
   - Full namenpm
   - National ID number
   - Date of birth
   - Phone number
   - Physical and postal address
   - Occupation and employer details
4. Upload required documents:
   - National ID (front)
   - National ID (back)
   - Passport photo
5. Submit your application
6. Wait for admin approval (typically 1-3 business days)
7. Receive notification when your account is activated

**Referral Program:**
- Use a referral code from an existing member to earn loyalty points
- Both you and the referrer earn 100 points upon successful activation
- Points can be redeemed for benefits

**Member Number:**
- Upon approval, you'll receive a unique member number (format: HRDC000001)
- Use this number for all SACCO transactions

**Account Status:**
- **PENDING_APPROVAL**: Application submitted, awaiting review
- **ACTIVE**: Account approved, full access to services
- **SUSPENDED**: Temporarily restricted access
- **INACTIVE**: Account dormant

## 2. SAVINGS & SHARES

### Types of Savings

**1. Share Capital:**
- Minimum shares required for loan eligibility: KES 5,000
- Shares represent your ownership stake in the SACCO
- Shares earn dividends based on SACCO performance
- Cannot be withdrawn while you have active loans

**2. Deposits:**
- Regular savings deposits
- No minimum deposit amount
- Can be made via:
  - M-Pesa
  - Bank transfer
  - Cash (at SACCO office)
  - Paystack integration

**Your Balance Structure:**
- **Total Shares**: Your share capital contributions
- **Total Deposits**: Your regular savings
- **Available Balance**: Total shares + deposits - committed balance
- **Committed Balance**: Amount pledged as guarantor for other members' loans

### How to Make Deposits

**M-Pesa:**

2. Select Lipa na M-Pesa → Paybill
3. Enter Paybill number: [SACCO Paybill]

5. Enter amount
6. Confirm with M-Pesa PIN
7. Deposit reflects within 5 minutes

**Bank Transfer:**
1. Use SACCO bank details provided in your dashboard
2. Reference: Your member number
3. Processing time: 1-2 business days

**Cash Deposits:**
- Visit SACCO office during business hours
- Receive official receipt
- Reflects immediately in your account

### Viewing Your Savings

- Log into your dashboard
- View real-time balance updates
- Download statements (coming soon)

## 3. LOANS

### Loan Eligibility

**Basic Requirements:**
- Active member status
- Minimum shares: KES 5,000
- Good standing with the SACCO
- No defaulted loans

**Loan Limits:**
- **Minimum loan**: KES 10,000
- **Maximum loan**: KES 5,000,000
- **Duration**: 3 to 60 months
- **Interest rate**: 12% per annum (default)

### Loan Types & Guarantor Requirements

**No Guarantors Required:**
- If loan amount ≤ Your available balance
- Example: Available balance = KES 150,000, Loan = KES 100,000 → No guarantors needed

**Guarantors Required:**
- If loan amount > Your available balance
- Number of guarantors = ceil((Loan Amount - Available Balance) / 100,000)
- Example: Available balance = KES 80,000, Loan = KES 250,000
  - Excess = KES 170,000
  - Guarantors needed = 2

### How to Apply for a Loan

**Step 1: Create Application**
1. Log into your dashboard
2. Navigate to "Loans" → "Apply for Loan"
3. Enter:
   - Loan amount (KES)
   - Duration (months)
   - Purpose of loan
4. View automatic calculation:
   - Monthly repayment
   - Total repayment
   - Total interest
5. Click "Create Loan Application"

**Step 2: Add Guarantors (if required)**
1. Go to your loan details page
2. Click "Add Guarantor"
3. Search for member by name or member number
4. Specify guaranteed amount
5. Send request
6. Wait for guarantor approval

**Step 3: Submit for Review**
1. Once all guarantors accept (if required)
2. Click "Submit for Review"
3. Loan enters approval workflow

**Step 4: Approval Process**
- **Under Credit Review**: Credit officer evaluates application (1-2 days)
- **Committee Review**: For high-value loans (2-5 days)
- **Approved**: Ready for disbursement
- **Disbursed**: Funds transferred to your account

### Loan Repayment

**Repayment Methods:**
- M-Pesa payments
- Bank transfers
- Direct debit (coming soon)
- Cash at SACCO office

**Repayment Schedule:**
- Monthly installments
- Payment due date: Same date each month
- Early repayment allowed with no penalties

**Late Payment:**
- Grace period: 5 days
- Late fees may apply after grace period
- Contact support if experiencing difficulties

### Loan Status Tracking

**Loan Statuses:**
1. **DRAFT**: Application created, not yet submitted
2. **SUBMITTED**: Under initial review
3. **AWAITING_GUARANTOR_APPROVAL**: Waiting for guarantor responses
4. **UNDER_CREDIT_REVIEW**: Credit officer evaluation
5. **COMMITTEE_REVIEW**: High-value loan committee approval
6. **APPROVED**: Loan approved, pending disbursement
7. **DISBURSED**: Funds released to member
8. **REJECTED**: Application declined (reason provided)
9. **CANCELLED**: Member cancelled application

**Loan Number Format:**
- Example: LN2026000001
- LN + Year + Sequential number

## 4. GUARANTORS

### Being a Guarantor

**What is a Guarantor?**
- A member who pledges their savings to support another member's loan
- Guarantors help members access loans beyond their savings
- Digital guarantor system with full transparency

**Guarantor Requirements:**
- Must be an active member
- Must have sufficient available balance
- Maximum commitment: 50% of your available balance
- Can guarantee multiple loans if balance permits

**How Guarantor Requests Work:**

**Receiving a Request:**
1. You receive a notification
2. View loan details:
   - Borrower information
   - Loan amount
   - Amount you're guaranteeing
   - Loan purpose
3. Check your available balance
4. Accept or reject with optional notes

**Your Commitment:**
- Guaranteed amount is "committed" from your available balance
- You cannot use committed funds for your own loans
- Commitment released when:
  - Loan is fully repaid
  - Loan is rejected/cancelled
  - Borrower defaults (you may need to cover the amount)

**Guarantor Responsibilities:**
- Ensure you trust the borrower
- Verify loan purpose is legitimate
- Monitor loan repayment status
- Be prepared to cover guaranteed amount if borrower defaults

**Declining a Request:**
- You have the right to decline any request
- No penalties for declining
- Provide optional reason for transparency

### Requesting Guarantors

**How to Find Guarantors:**
1. Identify members with sufficient savings
2. Approach them personally first (recommended)
3. Send digital request through the system
4. Provide clear loan purpose and repayment plan

**Best Practices:**
- Request from members who know you well
- Be transparent about loan purpose
- Demonstrate ability to repay
- Keep guarantors updated on repayment progress

**Guarantor Approval Timeline:**
- Guarantors have 7 days to respond
- Send reminders if needed
- Can replace non-responsive guarantors

**Eligibility Criteria:**

- Distributed proportionally to share capital

- No outstanding defaults

## 9. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

### General Questions

**Q: What is a SACCO?**
A: A SACCO (Savings and Credit Cooperative Organization) is a member-owned financial cooperative that provides savings and loan services to its members at competitive rates.

**Q: Is Kopa SACCO registered?**
A: Yes, Kopa SACCO is registered under the SACCO Societies Act and regulated by SASRA (SACCO Societies Regulatory Authority).

**Q: Are my savings safe?**
A: Yes, your savings are protected through SACCO regulations, insurance, and secure banking practices.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: No, each member can have only one account per National ID.

### Membership Questions

**Q: How long does approval take?**
A: Typically 1-3 business days after submitting complete documentation.

**Q: Can non-Kenyans join?**
A: Currently, membership is limited to Kenyan citizens with valid National IDs.

**Q: What if I don't have a referral code?**
A: Referral codes are optional. You can still join without one.

**Q: Can I close my account?**
A: Yes, after clearing all loans and providing 30 days notice.

### Savings Questions

**Q: Is there a minimum monthly deposit?**
A: No mandatory minimum, but regular savings are encouraged.

**Q: How do I earn dividends?**
A: Dividends are paid on share capital based on annual SACCO profits.

**Q: Can I transfer savings to another member?**
A: No, direct transfers between members are not allowed.

### Loan Questions

**Q: How fast can I get a loan?**
A: Simple loans (no guarantors): 1-3 days. Loans with guarantors: 3-7 days depending on guarantor response.

**Q: Can I repay my loan early?**
A: Yes, early repayment is allowed with no penalties.

**Q: What happens if I can't repay?**
A: Contact us immediately. We can discuss restructuring options before default.

**Q: Can I top up my existing loan?**
A: Yes, once you've repaid at least 50% of your current loan.

**Q: Why was my loan rejected?**
A: Common reasons include insufficient savings, incomplete documentation, or credit history concerns. Check rejection reason in your application.

### Guarantor Questions

**Q: What if my guarantor doesn't respond?**
A: After 7 days, you can request a different guarantor.

**Q: Can I remove a guarantor after approval?**
A: No, guarantors are locked once the loan is approved.

**Q: What if I guaranteed someone who defaulted?**
A: Your committed funds will be used to cover the default. You'll be notified before this happens.

**Q: How many people can I guarantee?**
A: No limit, as long as total commitments don't exceed 50% of your available balance.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions).

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: Mobile app is coming soon. Currently, use the mobile-responsive website.

**Q: How do I enable notifications?**
A: Allow notifications in your browser when prompted, or check browser settings.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
A: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset.

## 12. SECURITY & FRAUD PREVENTION

### Protecting Your Account

**Do's:**
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Log out after each session
- Verify website URL before logging in
- Keep your member number confidential
- Report suspicious activities immediately

**Don'ts:**
- Never share your password
- Don't use public Wi-Fi for transactions
- Don't click suspicious links in emails
- Don't share OTPs or verification codes
- Don't respond to unsolicited calls asking for credentials

### Recognizing Fraud

**Red Flags:**
- Calls claiming to be from SACCO asking for password
- Emails with suspicious links
- Requests to transfer money to "verify" account
- Promises of guaranteed loans for upfront fees
- Pressure to act immediately

**If You Suspect Fraud:**
1. Don't engage with the suspicious party
2. Contact Kopa SACCO immediately
3. Change your password
4. Report to authorities if necessary
5. Monitor your account for unauthorized activity

### Official Communication Channels

**Kopa SACCO Will:**
- Email from @kopasacco.co.ke domain only
- Call from registered phone numbers
- Never ask for passwords or PINs
- Provide official reference numbers
- Direct you to official website

**Kopa SACCO Will Never:**
- Ask for your password
- Request money to process loans
- Threaten immediate account closure
- Ask for remote access to your device
- Promise guaranteed loan approval for fees

## GLOSSARY OF TERMS

**Available Balance**: Total savings minus committed amounts (as guarantor)

**Committed Balance**: Amount pledged as guarantor for other members' loans

**Credit Review**: Evaluation of loan application by credit officer

**Dividend**: Share of SACCO profits distributed to members based on share capital

**Guarantor**: Member who pledges savings to support another member's loan

**Loan Number**: Unique identifier for each loan (format: LN2026000001)

**Member Number**: Unique identifier for each member (format: HRDC000001)

**Reducing Balance**: Loan calculation method where interest is charged on remaining principal

**Referral Code**: Unique code for inviting new members and earning rewards

**RLS (Row Level Security)**: Database security ensuring members see only their own data

**Share Capital**: Member's ownership stake in the SACCO

**Total Deposits**: Regular savings contributions

**Total Repayment**: Loan principal + total interest to be repaid
